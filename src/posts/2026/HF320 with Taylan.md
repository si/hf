<p>Taylan returns for another HF outing and wastes no time getting to it. From the swinging diplomacy of Embassy Of Swing to the wonky brilliance of Captain Wallop, this one's a proper showcase of the underground doing what it does best - groovy, weird, and utterly addictive.</p>
<p>There's UKG flavour from Jeremy Sylvester and James McKenzie, bass-heavy mischief from Malps, and a deep house closer from Cristi Cons and Leyah via Nervous Records. Sandwiched in between? A Bakey bootleg of a certified UK classic, Oldboy doing their thing on Swingers, and INSTINCT's essential remix of Trimmers by Frankel &amp; Harper tying things up.</p>
<p>If you like your house music with personality and a wink, this is the one.</p>
